📋 Deposit Instructions

Follow these steps to deposit USDT:

  1. Create/Log in to Your Binance Account: Ensure you have a verified Binance account before proceeding.
  2. Open Binance App & Scan QR Code: Launch the Binance app and scan the provided QR code to initiate the payment.
  3. Enter Deposit Amount: Minimum deposit is 5 USDT (required for subscription). You may deposit any amount above 5 USDT.
  4. Complete the Payment: Confirm and complete the payment securely through the Binance app.
  5. Submit Transaction ID: After payment, locate your Transaction ID (TxID) in Binance under payment details, and enter it in the required field on our platform.
  6. Confirm Deposit: Click the Deposit button and wait for confirmation.
  7. Verification & Status Update: Once we verify your payment, your deposit status will be updated within a few minutes.

⚠️ Important: Please enter a valid and correct Transaction ID (TxID). Do not submit fake or invalid transaction IDs. Any fraudulent deposit requests may result in account suspension or a permanent ban.
